About Me

My name is Marie Claire LeBlanc Flanagan. I design and build things for people. I build play experiences, installations, experimental video games, stories, communities, spaces, and events.

Why

I'm curious about people: how we express, how we connect, and how we experience the world around us. I enjoy making things that invite people to think in different ways, bring people together, and allow people to imagine different possibilities.

How

I work with people. Together we think about goals, values, interaction, motivation, friction, barriers, and flow. I listen to people and spend time thinking, experimenting, building things, and then listening again.

My Work

Co-operative and playful experiences

I make cooperative games, huge games for entire cities, and experimental games incorporating things like smell.
